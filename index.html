Friends of a Feather

A story-driven RPG about friendship, discovery, and courage.

About the Game

Friends of a Feather is a narrative RPG focused on boss battles, secrets, and emotional storytelling. You play as Doki, a brave bird helping Kino and his ghostly friend Gosty uncover the past and face the unknown.
